About

I am a PhD candidate in Philosophy at the University of Pittsburgh. I work in the philosophy of physics, the philosophy of science, metaphysics, and epistemology. My dissertation examines issues in quantum field theory and cosmology, with chapters on the interpretation of the Unruh effect, the Boltzmann brain problem, and indifference principles.
